About us Hotel Summit, NuwaraEliya

Hotel Summit lets you stay on top, naturally. Located 6,000 ft above sea level in the cool climes of the hill country of Sri Lanka, this exclusive mountain hideaway offers deluxe accommodation, superb international dining and full leisure facilities...

Set amidst forested hills and lush green tea plantations, this five star resort lets you unwind and relax as you breathe in the fresh air and get away from the daily grind by reading a book sprawled on an armchair munching on delectable sandwiches made from fresh ingredients or taking a stroll through a tea plantation or flower garden.

Indulge yourself at Hotel Summit, whether you are a family on vacation looking for good food, great accommodation and a little adventure, or honeymooners hoping to cosy up in the suite, ordering room service whenever you feel like it and basking in warmth as the cold mists roll around the hills outside. .
